Dita has a BFA in Design and Technology from Parsons School of Design and is completing their Certificate in Full Stack Desvelopment at Rice University. They explored physical computing, generative art and music, 3d modeling, game design, vr experiments, projection mapping, and soft circuitry.

Now they are focusing on marrying their love for creative technology with web development.

Having grown up on mmorpgs, Dita is most interested in the effects virtual worlds have on our identities and the communities that manifest in those spaces.
